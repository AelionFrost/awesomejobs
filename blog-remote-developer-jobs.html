Top 10 Remote Developer Jobs in 2026

June 14, 2026 · Development

Software engineering continues to lead the remote work market. From frontend to AI engineering, here are the most in-demand developer roles and what they pay in 2026.

1. AI/ML Engineer

The AI boom has created unprecedented demand. Companies like Anthropic, OpenAI, and Scale AI are hiring remote AI engineers at premium salaries — often 20-30% above general software roles.

2. Full-Stack Developer

Versatility pays. Full-stack developers who can work across the entire tech stack remain the most hired role on AwesomeJobs — over 1,500 active listings.

3. Platform Engineer

Companies need specialists to build internal developer tools and infrastructure. Platform engineers at Stripe, Datadog, and Cloudflare command $180k+.

4–10: Backend, Frontend, DevOps, Security, Mobile, Data, QA

Backend engineers ($130k-250k), frontend specialists ($100k-200k), DevOps/SRE ($130k-280k), security engineers ($120k-260k), mobile developers ($110k-220k), data engineers ($120k-250k), and QA automation engineers ($90k-180k) round out the top 10.
