Best Companies Hiring Remotely in 2026

June 14, 2026 · Companies

The remote work revolution is led by companies that have fully embraced distributed teams. These companies do not just allow remote work — they are built for it. Here are the top remote-first and remote-friendly employers hiring right now.

Fully Remote Companies

  • GitLab — 2,000+ employees across 65 countries. The gold standard for remote culture.
  • Automattic — the company behind WordPress.com. Fully distributed since day one.
  • Zapier — automation platform with 800+ employees, all remote.
  • Buffer — social media tools. Transparent salaries and fully remote.
  • Doist — makers of Todoist and Twist. Async-first, global team.

Hybrid and Remote-Friendly

Companies like Stripe, Shopify, Airbnb, Spotify, and Dropbox offer extensive remote options with competitive compensation. These companies have 500+ remote job listings active right now on AwesomeJobs.

What to Look For

When evaluating remote companies, check if they have written documentation cultures, async communication practices, and clear remote compensation policies. Companies that treat remote as an afterthought rarely pay well or support career growth for distributed employees.
